Ishi Inu
Ishi Inu is the ancestor of Shiba Inu
Ishi, a Shiba Inu, arose as a powerful symbol of endurance and determination. Ishi's tale epitomizes the essence of the Shiba Inu breed, celebrated for its loyalty, intelligence, and unwavering resolve. Thanks to the dedication of breeders and enthusiasts, Ishi was acknowledged as the progenitor of all contemporary Shiba Inus, safeguarding the breed's rich heritage and ensuring its legacy for generations to come.
